# Automated Intelligence — Research Publication Series

This repository holds the full text of the four-segment research publication series on AI architecture authored by Michael Barnes. Each segment is also published as an open-access preprint on Zenodo and is filed with the U.S. Copyright Office where indicated.

The canonical filed artifacts are the Zenodo PDFs. This repository carries the markdown source of each published document for in-browser reading, full-text search, and discoverability.

## The Series

One orientation paper (Preamble) and three architectural contributions, each contribution published as an A/B pair: an accessible Explanatory Companion (A) and a formal Definition (B).

### Preamble

*A Preamble to Automated Intelligence, Authorization Topology, and Identity Continuity*

Names three structural distinctions current AI vocabulary does not carry: entity, authority, continuity. Establishes the questions the three contributions answer.

- File: [`preamble/preamble.md`](preamble/preamble.md)
- DOI: [10.5281/zenodo.20468026](https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.20468026)
- Published: May 30, 2026

## Structure

Each contribution segment is published as two documents:

- **Companion (A)** — accessible introduction. Builds the concept through familiar examples; orients the reader.
- **Definition (B)** — formal architectural definition. Version-locked, citation-grade.

A papers earn the necessity of B; B papers carry the formal structure.

## Citing
